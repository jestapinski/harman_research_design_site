The context of design

Folk Model of Autonomy

Through our research we found that there is a common miconception about autonomy: that it is binary. The prevailing idea is that the occupant of the vehicle is either not driving at all because the vehilce has taken over, or that the occupant is engaged fully in a manual driving task.

In reality, self driving is a mix of human supervision and assistive control provided by the vehicle. The occupant does not have the luxury of enjoying the activities one would in say their living room. In fact, they must be prepared to take over at anytime.
